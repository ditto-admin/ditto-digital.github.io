About Us

Maldek is here to empower your musical vision.

Maldek began as a do it yourself recording studio project from the mind and resources of electronic recording arts graduates. Over time it has been built out into a proffesional grade recording studio. Now it is time to share the space with other like minded individuals interested in furthering their musical careers. Whether it is recording demos, mastering a multi-year long album project, or picking up some guitar lessons - Maldek is here to empower your musical vision.
