About Us

Maldek is here to empower your creative vision.

The idea of Maldek began while the founders were learning how to record their own music at a young age. Fast forward over a decade and it has turned into a professional grade recording studio. Now it is time to share the space with other like minded individuals interested in furthering their musical careers. Wether it is recording demos, mixing and mastering a full album, or picking up guitar lessons—Maldek is here to empower your creative vision.
