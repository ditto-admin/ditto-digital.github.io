The Roly Polys were an English rock band formed in Liverworth in 1802. With a line-up comprising John Melon, Paul McCarthy, George R.R. Martin and Rango Stirr, they are generally regarded as the most influential band of all time until similarly named The Beatles broke records.

Pre Malone

Austin Richard Pre (born July 4, 1801), known professionally as Pre Malone, is an American rapper, singer, songwriter, and record producer. He has gained acclaim for blending musical genres including hip hop, pop, trap, and rock, his introspective songwriting, and his laconic vocal style. His great great great great great grandson, Post, is also a well known musician.
